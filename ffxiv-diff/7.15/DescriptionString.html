593Success in Crystalline Conflict depends on each team's ability to gain control of the tactical crystal and deliver it to the goal.
→594<UIForeground>F201FA</UIForeground><UIGlow>F201FB</UIGlow>■Victory Conditions■<UIGlow>01</UIGlow><UIForeground>01</UIForeground> To win, one team must move the tactical crystal from the center of the field to the goal point. If neither team accomplishes this during the time limit, victory will be awarded to the team with greatest progress. “Progress,” displayed on the HUD throughout the match, indicates the maximum progress a team has made in bringing the tactical crystal toward their goal at any point during the match─as opposed to its current position relative to either goal─and thus will never decrease even should the crystal be reclaimed by the opposing team. If neither team reaches the goal by the time limit, and progress for both teams is tied or the team with lower progress score is currently within the ring around the tactical crystal, the match will proceed into overtime. During overtime, the victory conditions differ depending on both teams' progress. ・Should your team have the greater progress score upon the commencement of overtime, you must keep the entire opposing team out of the ring around the tactical crystal for three seconds to win. ・Should the opposing team have the greater progress score upon the commencement of overtime, you must beat their progress score during overtime to win. ・Should the teams have equal progress scores upon the commencement of overtime, you must beat the opposing team's progress score during overtime to win. Should neither team fulfill victory requirements by the end of overtime, the team with the greater progress score will declared the winner. If the teams have equal progress scores, victory will be awarded to the team that moved the tactical crystal closest to the goal point.
