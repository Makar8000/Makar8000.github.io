4.4005. Order of PlayEach of the four players begins with 13 tiles, and the dealer─who is chosen automatically─plays first. That player draws one new tile and chooses one tile to discard. This may either be the new tile or one from their hand. Once that player has discarded a tile, the player to the right takes a turn. This process is repeated until the end of the hand.The hand ends when a player calls a winning hand. The hand also ends if all of the tiles are drawn with no player calling a winning hand.ui/icon/086000/086007.texAt the start of a new hand, the role of dealer passes to the player on the right. In a full match, a round consists of eight hands, during which time the role of dealer passes around the board twice. The player with the highest score at the end of the round is declared the winner. In a quick match, only four hands are played. 0
