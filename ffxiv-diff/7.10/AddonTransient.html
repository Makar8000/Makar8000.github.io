→1072→Interior Design
→1073→Renovate an estate's interior with the interior design feature. From the Social menu, select “Housing,” followed by “Interior Design” to open the relevant window, from which you may select the desired interior style. Once applied, everything inside of the estate aside from the wallpaper, flooring, and ceiling lights will be changed. Please note that the floor layout, including the position of the stairs, may also be altered.

→1076→Authorization
→1077→Adjustment of an estate's interior design can only be performed by those granted the necessary authorization. Free Company estates: Members who have been granted both hall remodeling and furnishing placement authorization may change the estate's interior design. Private estates: In addition to the owners, tenants with authorization to add, remove, or change the position/color of estate furnishings or walls may change the estate's interior design.
