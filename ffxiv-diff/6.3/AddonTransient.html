1046Prime collectable items that have reached maximum collectability will, on occasion, yield exceedingly rare items from aetherial reduction. Moreover, these prime collectables may also become sublime when they are gathered with maximum collectability, which guarantees a yield of rare items from aetherial reduction. A higher gathering rate increases your chances of a prime collectable becoming sublime.
→1047→Collectability
→1048→The collectability of an item is another means of measuring its intrinsic quality. Collectability required to deliver an item can be confirmed at the top right of the interface. When breaking down items via aetherial reduction, this rating indicates the collectability required to obtain items other than crystals.
